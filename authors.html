Meet the Minds Behind the Magic

Click on an author card to see their spotlight animation.

J.K. Rowling

Known for the Harry Potter series, her books have inspired millions and shaped modern fantasy. She built a world of magic, friendship, and courage that still feels like home to many readers.

George Orwell

Famous for ‘1984’ and ‘Animal Farm’, Orwell wrote powerfully about truth and freedom. His stories remind us to question authority, stay aware, and value honesty.

Jane Austen

A master of wit and romance, Austen explored relationships, class, and expectations with sharp humour. Her stories still make readers smile and think even today.
